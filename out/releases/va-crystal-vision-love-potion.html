Released on March 28, 2025

Recovered from a damaged hard drive in the fall of 2024, the original Crystal Vision 'found mix' was composed fifteen years ago for a wedding. 'Crystal Vision Love Potion' features the original tune alongside three fresh interpretations. The results are jungly and deep and primed for your local club or seance.
