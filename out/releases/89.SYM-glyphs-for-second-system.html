Glyphs For Second System

by 89.SYM

☺

Bandcamp

Soundcloud

Released on April 22, 2022

"Glyphs for Second System" is the first release by 89.SYM. It consists of on-grid dance tracks with the drums ripped out, field recordings and single takes, cut-up samples, drones, and autonomic melodies pulled from nostalgic harmonic memories and dreams about extraterrestrials.

Recorded and produced in different contexts over the past year, “Glyphs for Second System” is drum-less music centered on keyboard improvisation and a devotion to studio chance.
