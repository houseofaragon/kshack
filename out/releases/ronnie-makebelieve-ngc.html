Released on April 12, 2023

Astronomers and amateurs interested in observing deep-sky objects know: 'NGC' is the New General Catalogue. For production duo Ronnie Makebelieve, NGC signifies a new direction for their second public release. This collection includes elements of leftfield techno, bass, acid house music, and a damaging DnB workout remix by Dwarde.

Ronnie Makebelieve is the production duo of Mike Sheffield and Jeremy Krinsley, New York City artists who have released music under various monikers for imprints including Godmode, Dull Tools, and Fire Talk. They founded the kshack imprint together in early 2021.

Remixer Dinn Warde, aka Dwarde, is an electronic music artist and jungle producer from Darlington, England. He has released music on 7th Storey Projects, Future Retro London, Beyond Electronix, Distant Planet, and as part of the Simpsons-themed project Globex Corp alongside longtime collaborator Tim Reaper.
