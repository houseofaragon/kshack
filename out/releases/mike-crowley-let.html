Released on January 13, 2023

'Let' is the first EP by Mike Crowley. It features four original tracks and a remix by Guilt Attendant.

The EP emerged from Crowley's interest in somatics, or the study of dance through autonomic, body-centric movement. Producing the tracks involved the same process of stepping away from self and letting sounds be.

The album art is from Nikolai Bernstein's socialist research into industrial movement during production processes, which was generated with an early time-lapse technique that used tiny strobelights.

Additional mixing by Maxime Robillard
