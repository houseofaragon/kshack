Julienne

Released on April 26, 2024

For their third release, Sleep 300 detours from live recordings into carefully woven techno. 'Julienne' opens with a spoken sci-fi dj tool and follows the thread through three peak-hour studies in dance maximalism, tying it up with a warbled ambient outro, as a little treat.
