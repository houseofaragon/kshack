Chronic Fatigue

by Chronic Fatigue

☺

Bandcamp

Soundcloud

Produced by Chronic Fatigue, Mike Sheffield and Jeremy Krinsley

Mastered by Stephan Mathieu

Album Art by Hope Fitton

Released on January 26, 2024

Chronic Fatigue’s self-titled debut synthesizes poetry and machine music. The duo employ a minimalist approach, fabricating a rich aural tapestry with vocals and a single Korg Volca Beats.

The EP features a techno remix of “Paranoia” reconstructed by MAL.E (a.k.a. Sweater on Polo) reminiscent of early Robert Hood compositions from his Detroit imprint M-plant.

Purchase of the EP includes a lyrics booklet designed by Chronic Fatigue.
