What Is Soap?

by Greem Jellyfish

Album Art by Greem Jellyfish

Released on March 29, 2024

Welcome to Greem Jellyfish's latest EP, which answers the eternal question: What is soap?

The release includes remixes by Costin Rp, EscaFlowne, Lightcyan, and 504 Aquatic, as well as original artwork by Greem Jellyfish.

Greem Jellyfish is a visual artist, DJ, and composer raised in Seoul and based in New York. Her work explores the boundaries between play, sensuality, paradox, and bubbles.
