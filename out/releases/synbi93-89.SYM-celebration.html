'celebrations' is a dub/ambient collaboration between the right and left neocortices of kshack co-founder Jeremy K. They released the first album by 89.SYM in 2022 to a bit of love ('mesmerising', said Boomkat). They pass the project like a party joint to synbi93, a new home for these autonomic abstractions and personal explorations.
